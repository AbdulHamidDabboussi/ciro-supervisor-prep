**Vendor discrepancy flag:** one third-party prep site (SeeWhy Learning) claims "110 questions, 150 minutes," which contradicts the official 90-question practice form and the syllabus element totals (10+10+9+20+15+8+6+7+5 = 90). Official figure: **90 questions / 3 hours**.

**Syllabus (companion document):** "Supervisor Exam Syllabus," last updated May 29, 2025 — https://www.ciro.ca/media/13606/download?attachment=. The syllabus's 9 elements with indicative question counts: 1 General regulatory framework — 10; 2 Supervisory structure: Investment Dealer responsibilities — 10; 3 Business and operations — 9; 4 Account approvals — 20; 5 Account activity — 15; 6 Activities of Approved Persons — 8; 7 Trading and market rules (incl. UMIR obligations) — 6; 8 Advertisements, sales literature and communications and research reports — 7; 9 Risks associated with Investment Dealer activity and registered locations — 5. Each learning outcome uses a Remember/Understand/Apply/Analyze taxonomy with "parameters." CIRO states the syllabus "is the authoritative list of what can be tested." The syllabus maps to the Supervisor competency profile (Appendix 16, https://www.ciro.ca/media/13631/download?inline=) rather than to named textbook readings; the companion "Guide for Studying" (https://www.ciro.ca/media/13671/download?attachment=) directs self-study candidates to the free primary sources (IDPC Rules, UMIR, related guidance, National Instruments).

## PART 4 — CONTENT-TO-RULES MAPPING STATEMENTS

- CIRO's official position: each syllabus "reflects the related competency profile, developed through a rigorous job task analysis"; the Supervisor competency profile (single profile for all Supervisor types) is the bridge between exam content and the rules. Reference document: "APPENDIX 16 — Reference Document for: Supervisor Competencies" — https://www.ciro.ca/media/13631/download?inline= ; Competency Hub: https://www.ciro.ca/registered-individuals/proficiency/competency-hub
- Implementing bulletin (April 2025): https://www.ciro.ca/newsroom/publications/ciro-proficiency-model-approved-persons-under-investment-dealer-and-partially-consolidated-rules-0 (amendments to Rules 2500–2800; effective Jan 1, 2026; Appendix 1 clean rules at ciro.ca/media/12626, Appendix 5 table guide at ciro.ca/media/12621)
- July 21, 2025 bulletin announcing Supervisor syllabus/materials: https://www.ciro.ca/newsroom/publications/ciro-proficiency-model-approved-persons-under-investment-dealer-and-partially-consolidated-rules-2
- Supervisor approval prerequisites under amended Rule 2600: §2603 pre-approval proficiencies (Supervisor Exam; CIRE for certain Supervisors with exceptions; product exams such as the Derivatives Exam where supervising derivatives; 2-year relevant experience), §2604 post-approval, §§2625–2628 exemptions/validity (per CIRO's Guide to Investment Dealer Categories, April 1, 2026 version)
- No page-by-page "reference readings" list exists in the syllabus; the syllabus uses learning outcomes + parameters as the authoritative scope, and the Guide for Studying points to the primary sources (IDPC Rules, UMIR, GNs, NIs) — all free on ciro.ca/osc.ca
